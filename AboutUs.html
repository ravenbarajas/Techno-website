About Us

Our Company

Our business operates an online food and beverage ecommerce platform with the aim of creating a thriving community made up of happy customers and small, high-quality food and beverage suppliers.                                                                                      Our company’s competitive advantage lies within the members of this organization that are purpose-driven in order to bring quality service that are better, faster, and cheaper than the existing competitors.


Our Mission & Vision Statement

Our company’s mission is to build a food loving community centered on improving small, micro, and medium food and beverage enterprises while providing a safe and excellent tasting food at a reasonable price.

Our vision is to be the leading food & beverage ecommerce platform in the country and build a huge community of businesses and clients in the process.


Our Company's Future

Our company’s future goal is to scale and compete with the largest food and beverage companies in the country by utilizing our technological and manpower resources to build a successful unicorn startup in the food and beverage startup space.
